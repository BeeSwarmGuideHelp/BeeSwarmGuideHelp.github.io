How to Macro in Bee Swarm Simulator

Installation and Setup:

  • System Requirements: You'll need a PC or laptop running Windows.
  • Discord Account: Ensure you have a Discord account ready as we'll be using it for macro installation.
  • Join Natro Macro Discord: Head over to the Natro Macro Discord server. Joining is straightforward, and it's where you'll find the macro files.
  • Download Macro: Navigate to the Natro channel and download the latest version of the macro.

Installation Process:

  • Extract Files: Once downloaded, extract the files to a location of your choice, such as your desktop.
  • Run Macro: Double-click on the extracted folder and then double-click on 'start' to launch the Natro Macro.

Configuration:

  • Choose Your Field: Select the field you want to macro in. For example, Blue Hive players can opt for the Pine Tree field.
  • Pattern Shape: ELO pattern is the easiest. Choose your desired pattern shape.
  • Gather Setting: Opt for 'Hive by walk' if you don't have much instant conversion. This setting ensures better honey retention.
  • Collect/Kill Options: Configure whether you want the macro to collect or kill certain mobs. Adjust settings based on your requirements.
  • Dispensers: Enable dispensers like glue and royal jelly as per your preferences.
  • Boost Settings: Set up boosts such as extracts to enhance gathering efficiency.
  • Quests: Enable quests like polar bear quests for additional benefits.
  • Planners: Configure planners to automate tasks like nectar collection efficiently.

Advanced Features:

  • Status Update: Utilize Discord integration for real-time updates on your honey production and other stats.
  • Customization: Fine-tune settings according to your playstyle and hive requirements.

Starting and Stopping the Macro:

  • Start Macro: Press F1 to initiate the macro. Sit back and watch as the macro handles the grind for you.
  • Stop Macro: Press F3 to halt the macro when you're done.
